Rooms

Cozy Stay Hostel offers a variety of room options, including shared dormitories and private rooms, to cater to different guest preferences. Each room is designed with comfort and functionality in mind, featuring comfortable beds, clean linens, and personal lockers for secure storage. The dorms are equipped with bunk beds, individual reading lights, and privacy curtains, providing a cozy and private space for each guest.



Aminities

  • Free Wi-Fi: High-speed internet is available throughout the property, ensuring guests stay connected whether they are working remotely or planning their next adventure.
  • Kitchen: Guests can enjoy the use of a fully-equipped shared kitchen where they can prepare meals, store groceries, and meet fellow travelers.
  • Common Areas: The hostel boasts spacious common areas, including a lounge with comfortable seating, a library corner, and a rooftop terrace that offers stunning views of the city skyline.
  • Laundry Facilities: Self-service laundry facilities are available for guests, making long-term stays convenient.
  • 24/7 Reception: The hostel provides 24-hour reception and security, ensuring guests feel safe and supported at all times.
